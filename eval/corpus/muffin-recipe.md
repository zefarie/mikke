# Blueberry muffins, small batch

Makes eight muffins. Whisk 210 g flour, 100 g sugar, two teaspoons baking
powder and a pinch of salt. In another bowl, combine one egg, 120 ml milk,
80 g melted butter and a teaspoon of vanilla.

Fold the wet mix into the dry ingredients — stop while the batter is still
lumpy, overmixing makes muffins tough. Gently fold in 150 g blueberries
(frozen work fine, do not thaw). Fill the cases, sprinkle with coarse sugar
and bake 22 minutes at 190 °C. Cool ten minutes before unmolding.
