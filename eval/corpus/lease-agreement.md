# Residential Lease Agreement

This lease covers the one-bedroom apartment at 44 Alder Street, unit 3B.
The term runs twelve months starting May 1st, 2026.

**Monthly rent is $1,150**, due on the first of each month. A security
deposit of one month's rent is held in escrow and returned within 30 days
of move-out, less any deductions for damages beyond normal wear and tear.
Tenant pays electricity and internet; water and trash are included. Subletting
requires the landlord's written consent. Either party may terminate at the
end of the term with 60 days' written notice.
