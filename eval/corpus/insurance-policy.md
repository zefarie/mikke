# Auto Insurance Policy — Summary of Coverage

Policy FT-2231-98 covers the insured vehicle for the period March 2026 to
March 2027.

- **Collision coverage**: repairs to your car after an accident, regardless
  of fault. Deductible: $500.
- **Liability**: bodily injury and property damage caused to third parties,
  up to $300,000 per incident.
- **Comprehensive**: theft, fire, hail, falling objects. Deductible: $250.
- Roadside assistance and rental reimbursement included.

Claims must be reported within 72 hours of the incident via the mobile app
or the 24/7 claims hotline.
