# Smartphone Limited Warranty — 24 months

This warranty covers manufacturing defects in materials and workmanship for
twenty-four months from the date of purchase shown on your receipt.

What is covered: battery failing below 80% health within the first year,
screen defects (dead pixels, ghost touch), charging port failure, speaker
and microphone defects.

What is not covered: accidental damage, liquid ingress, unauthorized
repairs, cosmetic wear. To make a claim, back up your data, then contact
support with your proof of purchase and the device serial number.
